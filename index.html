Frequently Asked Questions:

Is it Free?

Yes, it is and always will be :)

What languages can I practice?

Currently, we match people learning English, Spanish, French and Russian languages. We expand the list as we see enough language representatives on the platform

I'm a beginner, should I use it?

Answer is not easy but lean to no. We recommend people learn some language basics to actually be able to speak (even using limited vocabulary and do not pay attention on grammar) otherwise conversation won't be efficient neither for you nor for your peers

Can I find English teacher here?

You can find a lot of interesting folks but none of them will be dedicated teacher for you. The idea of the platform is just Talk with different people without thinking about learning process and fear of mistakes (like you did when you was a kid)

Will I speak with natives?

Although we do not guarantee each conversation has native speakers, we encourage our users to join Talk as native speakers from time to time. So the platform becomes online language exchange platform but not limited to it
